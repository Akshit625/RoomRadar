About RoomRadar

We help students find practical, trustworthy places to stay near campus.

RoomRadar brings map-based discovery, direct owner contact, role-based workflows, and local context into one student-first housing experience.

Explore properties List your property

What we focus on

The product is designed around how students actually search for housing.

Location first Map search Students can search around real campus corridors and neighborhoods.
Clarity first Useful filters Price, property type, listing mode, and area all stay easy to compare.
Trust first Verification Owners submit listings and admins help review marketplace quality.
Local first Dehradun The current demo is centered around Dehradun student housing clusters.

Our Mission

Make student housing discovery simpler, faster, and more transparent.

Students should not have to jump between incomplete posts, vague map pins, and scattered chat threads just to compare a few realistic options near campus. RoomRadar is built to make that search more focused and less stressful.

How RoomRadar Works

Each role gets tools designed for the part they play.

Students

Explore listings on the map and contact owners directly.

Owners

Create, update, and manage listings while tracking inquiries.

Admins

Review submissions, maintain listing quality, and monitor activity.

Current Scope

The seeded dataset is tailored to Dehradun.

The current map experience highlights Dehradun areas near Clement Town, Graphic Era University, and Graphic Era Hill University so the demo stays locally relevant and easy to evaluate.

Why It Matters

Housing decisions affect commute, budget, and everyday student life.

A better search experience helps students make faster, more confident choices and helps owners present their properties in a clearer, more structured way.
